How it works

Connect your Strava account by clicking the button above.

You'll then be able to view a report containing stats and charts for your top Strava activities. See your average speed, max length, performance vs time of day, and more.

A few notes:

  • You will only see stats for activities that have at least 5 entries.
  • Stats will be generated for at most three activities.
  • The connection to Strava will not work if you use Google, or another 3rd party, to log in to Strava.
